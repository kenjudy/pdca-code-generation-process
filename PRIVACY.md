# Privacy Policy

**PDCA Framework**
Last updated: April 2026

## Summary

PDCA Framework collects no data. It is a prompt skill that runs entirely within your Claude environment.

## What We Collect

Nothing. The PDCA Framework skill does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. It has no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, and no network connections of its own.

## How It Works

The skill provides structured prompts to Claude. All processing happens within your existing Claude session (Claude.ai or Claude Code). Any data you share in your Claude session is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.
